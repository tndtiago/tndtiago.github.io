Heart

Blood pressure monitor


Goals

The goal of this free project was to identify an issue in order to solve it. Here, the issue is, in a way, the nature of the object. Being a medical appliance, this object often doesn’t visually fit in a modern house environment. This is a problem because most often it is left outside, on top of a table, for example. Thus, the main goal of this project was to better the aesthetic of the object without taking away any of its practicality.
